The Art of Architecture

Architecture is one of humanity's oldest and most powerful forms of art that consist of designing and constructing spaces that shape how we live, work, and experience the world around us. Architecture reflects the values, technology, and ambitions of its era. The Empire State Building, completed in 1931 in New York City, stands as one of the most iconic examples of Art Deco architecture — a testament to human ingenuity and the desire to reach ever greater heights. Great architecture does more than shelter; it inspires, endures, and tells the story of civilisation itself.
